Why Pragma Works

Before Pragma: Sales teams rely on guessing. They send one email to one contact. If there's silence, they don't know if the blocker is technical (CTO), financial (CFO), product (CPO), or legal. They send more generic emails. The deal dies.

After Pragma: The system discovers high-intent prospects automatically. Scores them by both fit (WHO) and timing (WHEN). Generates persona-specific emails that address each stakeholder's actual concerns—not generic pitches. Monitors every step of activation and auto-diagnoses why deals stall.
