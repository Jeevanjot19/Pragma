WHEN Layer

Temporal Recency Scoring (The Secret Sauce)

Innovation: Signal decay via exponential half-life (exp(-days/14)). A funding signal from today is 3.7× more valuable than the same signal from 3 weeks ago. Tracks 100+ monitoring events and generates weekly priority lists. Automatically escalates stale deals (30+ days without action).

  • Exponential decay formula: signals lose 50% value every 14 days
  • 100+ monitoring events tracked per prospect (monthly granularity)
  • WHEN-score: Urgency & readiness 0–100 (time-aware)
  • Weekly action recommendations: "CALL THIS WEEK" or "EMAIL NEXT WEEK"
  • Auto-escalation if prospects stale >30 days without engagement
  • Event decay prevents false urgency from old signals

HOW Layer

4 engineered playbooks (CTO, CFO, CPO, Legal) with 3–4 escalating interventions each. Automates multi-stakeholder sequencing with mail-bombing prevention, weekend-aware scheduling, and per-contact engagement tracking. Prevents the "send one email and hope" problem.

  • 4 playbooks: CTO (technical fit), CFO (revenue impact), CPO (product synergy), Legal
  • 12+ intervention templates with per-prospect variable substitution
  • Campaign sequencing algorithm: CTO → CEO → CFO → Product users (role-aware)

Routes to appropriate teams with SLAs and escalates high-risk deals. Buyer committee sentiment tracking prevents silent drops.

  • 6 milestones: Integration → Sandbox → Production → First Txn → Volume → Recurring
  • Auto-diagnosis: 6 root cause categories (technical, compliance, business, integration, relationship, resource)

Why Pragma Wins

Why It Works: Built ground-up for India fintech. 15 RBI/SEBI compliance rules (Western GTM tools fail here).
