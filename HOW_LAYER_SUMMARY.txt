COMPLIANCE CHECKING SYSTEM
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Rule-based fintech compliance validator prevents sending emails that violate:

HIGH SEVERITY (BLOCKS email if triggered):
├─ Guaranteed returns ("guaranteed", "assured return", "fixed return")
├─ Incorrect DICGC statements ("100% safe", "fully insured")
├─ Fake RBI approval ("RBI approved", "RBI certified")
└─ Impossible regulatory promises ("guaranteed approval", "definitely compliant")

MEDIUM SEVERITY (WARNING, not blocked):
├─ Unqualified interest rates ("8.5% interest") — need "up to 8.5% p.a."
└─ Misleading competitor comparisons ("better than Stable Money")

Test Results:
─────────────
Good email (no triggers):     Status = CLEAR ✓
Bad email (multiple violations): Status = BLOCKED ✓
Warning email (rate unqualified): Status = WARNING ✓

PERSONA CUSTOMIZATION

Each persona gets customized email addressing their specific concerns:

CPO / Chief Product Officer
├─ Primary concern: Time to market, UX quality
├─ Values: White-label UI, fast go-live, success stories
├─ Proof point: "MobiKwik went live in under 2 weeks"

CFO / Chief Finance Officer
├─ Primary concern: Cost, revenue model, regulatory risk
├─ Values: Commission economics, compliance proof, insurance clarity
├─ Proof point: "Commission on every FD. DICGC insured to ₹5L"
└─ Fear: "Regulatory exposure or unclear revenue"
