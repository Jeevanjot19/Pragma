The System

Four layers of intelligence,
one unified dashboard.

Most GTM tools solve one problem. Pragma connects discovery, prioritisation, outreach, and partner health into a single coherent workflow.

Layer 01 · WHO
Prospect Discovery
& Fit Scoring
Pragma continuously monitors 16 targeted Google News feeds and scores every company it finds on a 0–100 scale. No manual research. No spreadsheets.
  • Funding rounds, leadership hires, product launches — each signal carries specific weight in the WHO score
  • Play Store enrichment: install count, product flags (has FD? has bonds?) detected automatically
  • Category classification: lending vs. neobank vs. wealth management changes which Blostem product gets recommended
  • Competitor displacement detection: if they use Stable Money or Deciml, they're a higher-priority target
  • Revenue proof calculator built in: click any prospect, see projected Year 1 commission in seconds
Layer 02 · WHEN
Temporal Scoring
& Weekly Priorities
A WHO score tells you who fits. WHEN tells you who is ready to hear from you right now — this Monday, not six months ago.
  • Exponential decay on event signals: a funding round from yesterday scores 40 pts, the same from 30 days ago scores 4
  • Contact factor: just reached out to someone? Their urgency score drops to 0.5× to prevent re-engagement spam
  • Five action buckets every week: CALL THIS WEEK → EMAIL → INTRO → NURTURE → MONITOR
  • Event boost by type: FUNDING and DISPLACEMENT carry the highest urgency multipliers
  • The dashboard becomes your Monday morning briefing: open it, see exactly who to contact today
Layer 03 · HOW
Persona-Specific
Outreach Generation
Select a prospect. Click Generate. Get three fully written, compliance-checked emails in under 10 seconds — one for the CPO, one for the CTO, one for the CFO.
  • Each email is genuinely different: the CTO email focuses on integration time and API quality; the CFO email focuses on commission economics
  • Real-time compliance scoring (0–100) with live warnings as you type — no RBI-prohibited phrases slip through
  • "Enhance with AI" button expands a draft email from 400 to 600+ words with better structure and stronger CTAs
  • Full editor: customise recipient name, subject, body — all inline, before you send
  • 3-strike compliance override memory: if your team approves a rule exception three times, it stops blocking future sends
Layer 04 · ACTIVATE
Partner Activation
Health & Stall Detection
Most GTM tools end at the deal. Pragma keeps watching. If a partner goes quiet, it knows exactly why — and writes the intervention email for you.
  • Three stall patterns detected from real API call data: Dead on Arrival, Stuck in Sandbox, Production Blocked
  • Stall cards show the detection evidence: "0 API calls in 14 days" or "AUTH_FAILED error code on last sandbox call"
  • Intervention emails are pattern-specific: a technical stall gets a debugging email for the CTO; a procurement stall gets a business case email for the CFO
  • Intervention effectiveness metrics: track response rates and resolution rates per stall pattern over time
  • Political risk detection: build-vs-buy signals and competitor integrations flagged from news monitoring

Dead on Arrival
Zero API calls in 14 days after signing. The person who signed isn't the engineer. Pragma writes a getting-started email to the CTO.
Stuck in Sandbox
Sandbox calls exist, then silence. Error code from the last call is surfaced in the intervention email. The CTO gets the exact fix — not a generic "let's sync".
Production Blocked
Sandbox tests pass but no production calls for 14 days. An approval or procurement blocker. Pragma routes to the CFO — not the engineer.
